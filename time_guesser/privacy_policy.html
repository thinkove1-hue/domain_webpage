Time Guesser

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective date: June 13, 2026

Overview

Time Guesser ("the app", "we", "our") is a free mobile game developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the app and how it is used. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect your email, password, phone number, real name, or contact details.

To power the online leaderboard, the app creates an anonymous profile the first time you open it and stores the following on our backend (see “Online Leaderboard” below):

  • A random device identifier (a UUID generated on your device at install). It is not linked to your real identity or to other apps.
  • A display name, randomly generated by default (e.g. “swift_clock_42”). You may change it; if you enter your real name it will be visible to others, so we recommend a nickname.
  • Competitive gameplay records — for each ranked “Compete” round: the category, the target time, your accuracy score, and the time it was played.

Practice rounds and your local stats/history graphs are stored only on your device and are never sent to the backend.

Online Leaderboard

Your display name, average accuracy, number of attempts, and rank in each category are shown publicly on the in-app leaderboard to other players. Data is stored using Supabase, a third-party backend hosting provider, on our behalf.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. You can review Google's data practices at: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings:
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID

Consent (EEA, UK & Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the app will show a consent dialog on first launch (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform). You can choose whether to allow personalised ads. Your choice is respected at all times and can be changed by reinstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — backend hosting and database for the online leaderboard. Privacy Policy

Data Retention & Deletion

On your device: your local history and stats are deleted when you uninstall the app or tap “Clear history” in the Statistics screen. (This does not remove your leaderboard data.)

On our backend: your anonymous profile (device identifier and display name) and your Compete records are retained so the leaderboard works. To have this data permanently deleted, email us at thinkove@proton.me from the device, or include your display name, and we will remove it.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
