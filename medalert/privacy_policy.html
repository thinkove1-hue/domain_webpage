MedAlert

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective date: June 1, 2026

MedAlert does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how MedAlert ("the app", "we") handles information when you use our medication reminder application on Android or iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy — especially because our users include elderly individuals and their caregivers.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

MedAlert does not collect or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Medication names, schedules, or health data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data to external servers

There is no account registration, no login, and no backend server. The app works entirely offline.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All information you enter — medication names, doses, schedules, and history — is stored locally on your device only using an encrypted on-device database (Hive). This data never leaves your device unless you back it up through your phone's built-in backup system.

Deleting the app removes all stored data permanently.

3. Notifications

MedAlert uses your device's local notification system to send medication reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on-device. No notification content is sent to or processed by any external server.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device's system settings.

4. In-App Purchase

MedAlert offers a one-time unlock purchase ("MedAlert Pro") processed through Google Play Billing (Android) or the App Store (iOS). Payment is handled entirely by Google or Apple — we do not receive, store, or process any payment card details.

The only information we receive from the purchase is a confirmation that a valid purchase was made, which is stored locally on your device to unlock the app.

5. Third-Party Services

MedAlert uses the following platform services:

  • Google Play Billing — for in-app purchases on Android. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — for in-app purchases on iOS. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in the app.

6. Children's Privacy

MedAlert is designed for adults, including elderly users. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, there is no special risk to minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the new version at the same URL. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
